Oranga/Wellbeing Plan : Phase One

Hinengaro: Mental/Emotional

Doing things for my mental and emotion wellbeing, such as cleaning my room/cleaning my spaces for a clean and clearer mind. Moving slowly on my days off- allowing myself to relax my body. having long showers which makes me happy and clears my mind.

Tiana: Body/Psychical

Keeping up with my morning stretches: helping me stay intune with my body and help after hours spent sitting at a desk. Dancing! to move my body in a way that is fun for me. and going on walks, moving my body.

Wairua: Spiritual

Staying with my community; seeing my friends and maintaining my support circle with me. Listening to music to help soothe me. Journalling and reflecting to help collect my thoughts.

Whanau: Friends and Family

Making plans with friends, try leaving the house with friends or experiencing something together. calling friends/video calling to see their faces. Engaging in the same pastimes as friends.

Whena: Land/Roots

Eating healthy/eating vegetables, for my body and to connect with the land. Going for walks/allowing myself time to appreciate nature. mindfulness- to keep me present and intune with myself and the world around me.
